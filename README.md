## How to use the app
1. To edit a city and its province, click on a city in the view, then type the new city and province name in the popup box, then click OK.
2. You can also add new cities by clicking the plus button in the bottom right, typing in the name and province of the city, then clicking OK.
3. Clicking cancel on the popup boxes will cancel the current operation.
